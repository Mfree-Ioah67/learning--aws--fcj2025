### Key Highlights

#### About AWS First Cloud Journey Workforce Program

Launched in 2021, the program has accompanied over **2,000 students** nationwide:

- More than **150 trainees** have received in-depth training and are currently working at leading tech companies in Vietnam and internationally
- **Main objectives:**
  - Build a high-quality generation of AWS Builders for Vietnam
  - Equip hands-on skills in Cloud, DevOps, AI/ML, Security, Data & Analytics
  - Connect students with the AWS Study Group community of **47,000+ members** and AWS partner enterprises

The program is not just about technology training, but also serves as an important bridge between knowledge – technology – career, helping students confidently integrate into the modern tech world and international integration.

### Applying to Career Development

- **Start with Fundamentals**: Build strong foundation in AWS core services and cloud concepts
- **Get Certified**: Pursue AWS certifications (Cloud Practitioner → Associate → Professional)
- **Join the Community**: Actively participate in AWS Study Group (47,000+ members) for networking and learning
- **Hands-on Practice**: Work on real projects, contribute to open source, build portfolio
- **Learn from Alumni**: Connect with program graduates for mentorship and career guidance
- **Develop Soft Skills**: Communication, teamwork, and presentation skills are equally important
- **Stay Curious**: Follow industry trends in Cloud, DevOps, AI/ML, and emerging technologies
- **Network Actively**: Attend events, connect with professionals, build meaningful relationships
